Independent Quality Control Process

Every tool goes through a rigorous 8-step quality control process before it reaches you. We don’t just inspect — we guarantee consistency and reliability.

Our 8-Step Quality Control Process

1

Incoming Material Inspection

All raw materials and components are inspected upon arrival for quality and specification compliance.

2

In-Process Quality Checks

Multiple inspection points during assembly to catch issues early in the production process.

3

Functional Testing

Every tool undergoes full functional testing (torque, speed, battery performance, safety features).

4

Visual & Cosmetic Inspection

Detailed visual check for surface defects, labeling accuracy, and packaging quality.

5

Battery & Charger Testing

All batteries and chargers are tested for capacity, safety, and charging performance.

6

Random Sampling Audit

Independent quality team performs random batch audits to ensure consistency across production.

7

Final Pre-Shipment Inspection

Final comprehensive inspection before goods are packed and shipped to customers.

8

Documentation & Traceability

Full batch records and traceability maintained for every production lot.
