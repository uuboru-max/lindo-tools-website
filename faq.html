Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from our partners and customers.

General

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For standard wholesale orders, MOQ starts from 100–300 units depending on the product. For OEM and Private Label projects, MOQ typically ranges from 500–3000 units.

Do you offer samples before bulk orders?

Yes. We provide samples for evaluation. Sample cost and shipping fees apply and can be discussed case by case.

Battery Platform

Do all your tools use the same battery?

Yes. Most of our 18V/20V tools share the same battery platform, allowing customers to use one battery across multiple tools.

Can I order tools without batteries (bare tools)?

Yes. We offer both Kit versions (with battery + charger) and Bare Tool versions.

OEM & Private Label

What is the typical lead time for OEM projects?

From initial discussion to mass production, OEM projects usually take 4–8 months, depending on customization level and certification requirements.

Do you help with product certifications?

Yes. We coordinate testing and certification (CE, UL, RoHS, UN38.3, etc.) with accredited laboratories.

Shipping, Returns & Warranty

Do you offer tracked shipping?

Yes. All orders are shipped with tracked courier services. Tracking information is provided once the order is dispatched.

What is your warranty policy?

All products come with a 3-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects under normal use.

Can I return defective products?

Yes. We accept returns for defective items within 30 days of delivery. Please contact our support team for return instructions.
