Shipping, Returns & Warranty

Clear policies to support your business with confidence.

Shipping Policy

Delivery Time

Most orders are shipped within 3–7 business days after order confirmation. Delivery times vary by region and order volume.

Shipping Method

All orders are shipped with tracked courier services. We provide tracking information once the order is dispatched.

Bulk & Project Orders

For large volume or project orders, we offer dedicated logistics support and flexible delivery schedules. Please contact us for custom arrangements.

Returns Policy

Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for defective or incorrect items.

Condition for Returns

Products must be returned in original packaging and in unused condition (except for defective items).

How to Request a Return

Please contact our support team with your order number and reason for return. We will provide return instructions and authorization.

Warranty Policy

Standard Warranty

All PowerTools products come with a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering manufacturing defects under normal use.

What’s Covered

  • Defective materials and workmanship
  • Battery and charger defects (within warranty period)
  • Motor and electronic component failures

What’s Not Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage caused by misuse or improper maintenance
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications

How to Claim Warranty

Please contact our support team with your order number, product model, and description of the issue. We will guide you through the warranty claim process.
